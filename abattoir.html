abattoir

Slaughter Floor

We believe in humane slaughter and have implemented strict systems of maintenance and control that comply with the most stringent health standards.

Deboning Hall

Aside from traditional cuts we make optimal use of each animal by processing red and rough offal, as well as a range of value-added offal products that include tripe, liver and sweet meats. 

Cold Storage Facilty

We conduct ongoing audits and have a zero tolerance for sub-standard hygiene practices. As a result, we are able to minimise any risk of bacterial cross contamination throughout our abattoir.

Meat Processing

At our by-products plant we produce blood meal, carcass meal and tallow, which is used in pharmaceutical products and as additives for pet foods.

Packaging

To maintain the export status we enjoy, all health inspections during the slaughter process are carried out by the meat inspection division of the Department of Agriculture.
